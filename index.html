Regular Maintenance Cleaning

Your home deserves consistent care that preserves its calm and supports your busy life. Our regular maintenance cleaning service is designed for clients who value a perpetually fresh and orderly home without the weekly stress.

In Every Room, We:

  • Dust all accessible surfaces, furniture, shelves, and décor
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs; mop hard floors
  • Empty trash + recycling with liner replacement
  • Spot-clean fingerprints on mirrors and glass doors

Kitchen Focus:

  • Sanitize countertops + appliance surfaces
  • Clean sink + polish faucets
  • Wipe cabinet fronts + backsplash
  • Sweep + mop

Bathroom Focus:

  • Scrub toilet, sinks, shower, tub
  • Polish fixtures + clean mirrors
  • Wipe all surfaces + shelves
  • Empty trash, sweep + mop

Deep Cleaning Service
